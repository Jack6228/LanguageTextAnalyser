About Language Tribune


This site was created to allow learners of foreign languages to easily find texts in foreign languages online from song lyrics and news articles. All texts are then ranked by their difficulty so that users can find articles for their level of proficiency in a language.


The table in the 'Home' tab can be filtered to display texts across numerous fields:


Field Description
Language Currently supported languages:
Difficulty (easiest) 1 - 5 (hardest), languages are ranked individually
Album Album from which a song comes. For song lyrics only
Type Currently, only news articles ('Article') and song lyrics ('Song') are supported
Title Title of a text
Artist/Site Name of a song's artist or publisher of a news article

If there's an album, news article, news website, or even a new language that you want to be added to the database here, use the 'Request a Feature' button below to send a request.
